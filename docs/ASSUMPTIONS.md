# Assumptions

## Content & Data

- No internal Bitexen data systems are accessed — all content is based on publicly available information from bitexen.com, research.bitexen.com, and related public sources
- User numbers and trading volumes are industry estimates and publicly reported figures, not audited numbers
- ARPU projections are directional, based on comparable MVNO benchmarks in the South African market
- Revenue models are illustrative and require validation with actual commercial terms

## Strategic

- Bitexen will establish or designate a South African entity for MVNO licensing purposes
- The existing Bitexen technology platform (exchange, wallet, APIs) can be integrated with the MVNO platform via standard APIs
- Cell C or MTN would serve as the MNO host — commercial terms are not yet negotiated
- Digital Mobile or equivalent MVNE platform would provide the technical infrastructure
- Zero-rating Bitexen's own app on its own MVNO is structurally defensible under current ICASA regulatory guidance
- Crypto rewards and cashback programmes are compliant with South African financial regulations (SARB, FSCA)

## Scope

- This is a directional strategy proposal, not a binding commercial offer
- All pricing, tiers, and product structures require validation post-approval
- Technical feasibility of zero-rating and QoS prioritisation requires MNO engagement
- Go-to-market timelines are indicative and depend on MVNE platform readiness
- SA entity formation and ICASA licensing timeline is not included in the 8-week implementation roadmap

## Website

- This proposal site is a standalone marketing asset, not a production application
- No user authentication, payment processing, or personal data collection is included
- The site is intended for desktop and mobile viewing by a limited stakeholder audience
